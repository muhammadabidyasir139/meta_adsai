# Product Brief: Ads Machine Learning Monitoring Dashboard

## Executive Summary
For Indonesian MSMEs (UMKMs), digital advertising on platforms like Meta is often a high-stress "black box" where manual budgeting leads to either wasted spend or missed opportunities. The **Ads Machine Learning Monitoring Dashboard** transforms this process from a guessing game into a data-driven science. 

By integrating **Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA)** via IndoBERT and **Time-Series Demand Forecasting** (Prophet/LSTM), the system provides a "Decision Engine" that scores every product's readiness for ads. Users no longer need to analyze thousands of reviews or predict seasonal trends manually; they simply view the recommendations and provide a "One-Click Approval" to optimize their Meta Ads budget. This is a SaaS-based platform designed specifically to give small companies the same sophisticated budgeting power as large agencies, with a fraction of the effort.

## The Problem
Manual ad budgeting for small business owners is plagued by three major pains:
1.  **Complexity Fatigue:** Analyzing hundreds of product reviews to see if a product is "ready" for a high-spend campaign is exhausting and prone to human error.
2.  **Budget Anxiety:** UMKMs operate on thin margins. Every Rupiah spent on a low-performing ad is a direct hit to their livelihood.
3.  **Decision Paralysis:** Without clear forecasting, owners often stick to "safe" low budgets, missing out on massive growth during peak demand periods.

## The Solution
A web-based SaaS platform that serves as a command center for Meta Ads. 
*   **Ad Readiness Score (ARS):** A composite score derived from IndoBERT-powered sentiment analysis of product reviews, telling the user exactly which products are currently "winning" with customers.
*   **Demand Forecasting:** Predicting future sales trends using Prophet and LSTM models to suggest the optimal budget *before* the demand spike happens.
*   **The Decision Engine:** A semi-automated workflow where the system proposes budget changes (Maximize vs. Minimize) which the user can approve with a single click.

## What Makes This Different
Most global ad automation tools (Revealbot, Madgicx) use generic performance metrics (ROAS, CPA). Our **"Local Moat"** is the use of **IndoBERT**. By understanding the specific slang, sentiment, and nuances of the Indonesian language in marketplace reviews, our ARS is significantly more accurate for the local market than any global competitor. We don't just look at numbers; we look at the *voice of the customer*.

## Who This Serves
*   **Primary User:** Indonesian UMKM owners and small digital marketing teams who manage Meta Ads but lack the time or expertise for deep data analysis.
*   **Success for them looks like:** Spending less than 10 minutes a day on budgeting while seeing a measurable reduction in ad waste and an increase in profitable campaign scaling.

## Scope (MVP)
*   **In-Scope:** Meta Ads integration, IndoBERT sentiment analysis (ABSA), Prophet/LSTM forecasting models, "One-Click Approve" dashboard, and automated monitoring.
*   **Out-of-Scope:** Automated ad creative generation (images/copy), multi-platform support (TikTok/Shopee), and fully autonomous (zero-click) budgeting.

## Vision
In 2-3 years, this platform will become the "AI Growth Partner" for Indonesian commerce—expanding into all major marketplaces (Shopee, Tokopedia, TikTok) and evolving from a budgeting tool into a full-scale automated business strategist.
